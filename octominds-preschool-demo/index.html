Preschool ERP module map

The 7-module preschool version of the institutional system.

This is the scope behind the screens shown in the live demo.

1. Admissions and Enquiry Engine

Turn leads, campus visits, follow-ups, document collection, and enrolment into one visible conversion flow.

  • Lead capture by source and branch
  • Follow-up queue for counsellors
  • Campus visit scheduling
  • Admission confirmation and document status

2. Child, Parent and Classroom System

Create one child record that connects parents, class, centre, pickup permissions, medical notes, and classroom visibility.

  • Child and parent master records
  • Emergency contact and allergy flags
  • Program, class, and teacher mapping
  • Pickup authorization and sibling links

3. Progress and Development Tracking

Replace exam logic with developmental milestones, teacher notes, settling observations, and school readiness indicators.

  • Motor, language, and social-emotional progress
  • Monthly observations and readiness summaries
  • Parent-facing progress snapshots
  • Teacher-led milestone updates

4. Fees and Collection Control

Connect fee plans, due dates, receipts, concessions, branch-wise collections, and follow-ups to a single finance layer.

  • Program-wise fee plans and daycare add-ons
  • Pending dues and reminder automation
  • Receipts and payment method tracking
  • Leadership-level collection visibility by centre

5. Parent Communication App

Give parents one calm, trust-building app for updates, notices, events, receipts, leave, and child progress.

  • Daily updates and classroom highlights
  • Fee receipts and due alerts
  • Leave request and acknowledgement
  • Notice board and event reminders
  • Installable PWA on Android, iPhone, desktop, and tablet

6. Multi-Centre Admin and Compliance

Run branch checklists, staff records, visitor logs, incidents, pickup control, and SOP visibility from one back-office layer.

  • Branch audit and hygiene checklist
  • Staff attendance and leave records
  • Incident and safety logging
  • Admin escalations and branch support tasks

7. Central Leadership Dashboard

Show the school leadership team exactly what matters daily across all 7 centres without opening separate reports.

  • Admissions velocity and occupancy
  • Collections, defaulters, and fee health
  • Centre-wise staffing and attention areas
  • Live issue tracking and branch comparison

What the system connects

This child record links the class teacher, parent app, attendance, fee plan, pickup notes, and progress updates without duplicate entry.

  • One source of truth for the child
  • No separate fee register and parent list mismatch
  • Critical safety information always visible
  • Ready for parent report and class handover workflows

Teacher daily workflow

  • Mark attendance by class
  • Post classroom highlights and child notes
  • Update milestone observations weekly
  • Raise incident or health notes if needed

Why this matters operationally

  • Parent communication becomes structured, not ad hoc.
  • Teachers spend less time repeating the same updates across WhatsApp and registers.
  • Centre heads can review whether updates are actually going out on time.
