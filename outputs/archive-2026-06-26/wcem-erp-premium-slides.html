Why WCEM Needs This

WCEM already operates at a scale where a digital institution transformation system is no longer optional.

An autonomous, multi-program institution creates interconnected demands across admissions, academics, examinations, finance, placements, and compliance. Leadership needs one institutional system, not scattered tools, manual follow-up, and delayed visibility.

The need is not for “more software.” The need is for one system that gives WCEM operational control, faster decisions, and a stronger institutional experience.

Academic Complexity

BTech, MTech, Diploma, MBA, and MCA create multiple parallel workflows across departments and semesters.

Autonomous Operations

Public Service Layers

Leadership Visibility

Slide 35 · Lifetime Maintenance Framework

Lifetime free maintenance should sound strong, but still remain contractually safe.

SrS Logics can confidently commit to long-term free maintenance only when the maintenance boundary is commercially disciplined and technically explicit.
A

Definition of a Bug

Lifetime free maintenance applies strictly to code-level bug remediation. A bug means the system fails to execute a calculation, data logic, or signed workflow exactly as approved in the Final Discovery Blueprint. Any workflow change requested after blueprint sign-off becomes a change request and is separately scoped and billed.

Blueprint lock
B

Ecosystem Protection

SrS Logics warrants its own proprietary code. Free maintenance does not include recoding caused by RTMNU, AICTE, MSBTE, or MahaDBT rule changes, Apple iOS or Android web-engine shifts, or third-party API deprecations from SMS, WhatsApp, or payment providers.

External change shield
C

Data Sanity Firewall

Free maintenance covers the operational integrity of the software engine, not client-side data management. Database correction caused by manual staff mis-entry, corrupt CSV uploads, or unauthorized internal admin deletion is treated as emergency database intervention and handled under a separate incident fee.

Data protection boundary
D

Ticketing Governor

All free maintenance requests must move through the SrS Logics digital helpdesk ticket system. Standard SLA is 48 business hours for non-critical issues during Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Direct developer calls for non-system-down events are outside free maintenance coverage.

Support discipline
Free maintenance remains a strong long-term promise, but only within a disciplined support framework that protects both the institution and SrS Logics.
